Things to note before submiting

You need to scale your models accordingly. Your largest dimensions should be no greater than 200mm.
If you are unsure of your models scale, please use this tool to verify your models demisions. You may also use the tool to modify your model. If your model exceeds 25MB, you will promted with an error. To fix it, use this to reduce your file to less than 25MB. Note:reducing your files size will have no affect of your final result of final scale. You are simply reducing the quality of your file. Since 3D-Printers struggle with detail, this will have no affect on final results.

Naming your model a unique name is crucial for automation to take it's course. Once you are confident your model is properly scaled, name it something unique such as : 98657.stl or " a brief description of your project".stl. Avoid name like thing.stl or project.stl. 
ALL FILES MUST BE .STL FORMAT

Before you proceed you must consent to this and that you have read the instructions. OHS is not responsible for failed prints or wasted material due to misuse. Good luck printing!
