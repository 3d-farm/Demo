About

At OHS, we are passionate about making the world of 3D printing accessible to every student, unlocking the doors to creativity, innovation, and hands-on learning. Our mission is simple: to provide students with the opportunity to bring their ideas to life by offering free 3D printing services. We understand the importance of empowering the next generation of thinkers, creators, and problem-solvers, and we're thrilled to be part of your journey.

Submit your design

Upload your 3D design files to our form. Whether it's a project for school or a personal creation, we're here to turn your digital designs into tangible objects.

Printing Process

Our system will review your submission, and upon approval, your design will go through the 3D printing process. We use in-house developed technology to ensure consistent, highest quality prints.

Receive your models

Once your print is complete, we'll notify you via email. Enjoy the satisfaction of holding your creation in your hands, ready to showcase to the world.
